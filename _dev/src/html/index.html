Why Should You Chose ResilientDB?

JSince the inception (or rise) of Bitcoin, there has been a lot of interest in the design of new and efficient blockchain systems. At the core of any blockchain application is a Byzantine-Fault Tolerant (BFT) protocol that helps to replicate client transactions, consistently. Bitcoin and several other blockchain applications achieve consensus with the help of expensive protocols such as Proof-of-Work. Hence, they attain very low throughput and are computationally expensive. This led researchers to move towards the design of blockchain systems that employ classical BFT protocols, such as PBFT, to reach consensus. However, existing blockchain systems still attain low throughputs. So everyone blames this low throughput on the associated BFT protocol. We believe this is just one side of the story. Our ResilientDB fabric aims at "Making Permissioned Blockchain Systems Fast Again". ResilientDB includes parallel pipelines and well-researched design designs that help it to gain additional throughput.
